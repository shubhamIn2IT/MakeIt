CATS4U OFFERINGS

Stay updated and get things done with CATS4U

ProWatch

(Productivity Management)

ProWatch is an intelligent productivity management product that empowers organisations to optimizing resource utilization and formulate data driven plans to enhance overall operational efficiency

iDashboard

(Reporting & Analytics)

CATS iDashboard is an essential tool that helps businesses to make data-driven decisions by providing meaningful insights from multiple data sources. The tool provides a user-friendly interface with interactive dashboards and reports. The users can perform ad-hoc querying to explore the data further and gain insights. iDashboard supports self-service analytics, allowing users to access and analyze data on their own, reducing the burden on IT teams.

Mapula

(Virtual Agent)

CATS Mapula is chatbot framework which is a set of tools and technologies used to create intelligent conversational agents that can interact with users in natural language. It is meant for organizations looking to enhance their customer support and engagement capabilities. With features such as learning from experience, sentiment analysis, bot-to-human handoff, and voice recognition, a chatbot built on a robust framework can provide personalized and engaging interactions with users, leading to improved customer satisfaction and loyalty.

iServe

(Service Management)

CATS ITSM is a tool that provides IT teams with a comprehensive solution for managing IT services and infrastructure. With features such as an intuitive user interface, a powerful CMDB, customizable workflows, and integration capabilities, the tool enables IT teams to streamline their IT service delivery, improve service quality, and increase customer satisfaction.

iSense

(Intelligence Grid)

CATS EAA is a tool with AIOps capabilities that provides IT teams with a comprehensive solution for managing IT events and incidents. With features such as AIOps analysis, automation, collaboration, and optimized dashboards, the tool helps IT teams detect and resolve issues faster, reduce manual effort, and improve overall IT service delivery.

iCollect

(Billing Engine)

CATS iCollect is designed to help businesses to create and send invoices automatically, based on predefined billing cycles or customer contracts. It also allows businesses to customize invoices according to their branding and billing requirements, and it provides customers with an easy-to-understand breakdown of their charges. The engine also generates automated reminders for outstanding payments, reducing the risk of late payments and improving cash flow.

iProvision

(Smart Provisioning)

CATS iProvision is designed to simplify the process of configuring and managing network devices, reduce the risk of errors and improving the efficiency of IT operations. By automating provisioning and policy management, IT teams can focus on more strategic initiatives, while ensuring that their networks are secure, reliable, and optimized for performance.

iCustomer

(Integrated CRM)

CATS iCustomer is a comprehensive solution for businesses for managing their customer relationships and streamlining their sales processes. With features such as Sales Hub, Customer 360, Dashboard and reports, and Business Rules, iCustomer helps businesses improve sales efficiency, drive growth, and deliver better customer experiences.

iProcess

(Business Process Orchestration)

CATS iProcess is tool designed to stream workflows, improving efficiency, and reducing errors in an organization's business processes. It provides businesses with a competitive edge by enabling them to respond quickly to changing market conditions, while maintaining high standards of quality and compliance. It comes with intuitive and user-friendly interface for designing, executing, and monitoring workflows.
