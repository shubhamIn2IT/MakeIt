What is iDashboard?

iDashboard, the business intelligence and visualization solution that helps you understand your data in real-time and historical context. Developed by Cignera and offered by CATS4U, iDashboard provides a comprehensive view of your business performance with its user-friendly interface and powerful analytics features.

Real-time Insights, Customizable Reports, and Seamless Integrations!

Tired of manually collating data from different sources and systems just to measure your business performance? Look no further than iDashboard - the solution that helps you measure your business performance and make data-driven decisions in real-time.

iDashboards Features That Empower Your Data-Driven Decision-Making

Gain Deeper Insights and Improve Decision Making with Data Connectors, Business-Aligned Views, Real-Time Performance Monitoring, Anomalies Reporting, KPI-Driven Reports, Customizable Reports, Chatbot Integration, and Complex Charts with Drill-Down Functionality.
