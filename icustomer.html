What is iCustomer?

iCustomer is a powerful CRM solution designed to help businesses of all sizes manage their customer relationships with ease.
With a wide range of features and customization options, iCustomer is the perfect tool for businesses looking to
streamline their sales and customer service processes.

Get a solution that's tailor-made for you, not the other way around!

Pick and choose as you please! The iCustomer modules seamlessly integrate with your current
systems to create a complete solution that links every aspect of your business, helping you connect with every single customer.

Track leads, manage deals, and automate your sales process, all in one place.

  • Deal Management: Organize your sales pipeline with ease, from initial contact to closing the deal. Use deal stages and progress tracking to stay on top of your sales game.
  • Automation: Streamline your sales process with automated email sequences, and follow-up reminders.
  • Analytics: Gain insights into your sales performance with reporting and analytics. Track important metrics like revenue, deal size, and win rate.
  • Collaboration: Collaborate with your team to close deals faster, with features like team inbox, team activity feed, and deal assignments.
  • Integrations: Integrate with your tools and apps, including email clients, calendars, and more, to create a seamless sales workflow.

Consolidate customer data from multiple sources into a single, unified profile.

  • Unified Customer View: Get a complete view of all customer interactions, including communication history, purchases, support tickets, and more.
  • Data Management: Manage customer data efficiently by capturing, storing, and updating customer information in a central location.
  • Personalization: Use customer data to personalize interactions and deliver targeted messaging.
  • Reporting: Custom reports to track key metrics and gain insights into customer behavior.
  • CSAT Tracking: Track customer satisfaction and identify areas for improvement.

Centralized repository of all your products and services.

  • Customizable Catalog: Create and manage a comprehensive catalog of all your products and services with customizable attributes and pricing.
  • Bundling and Variants: Easily create bundles and variants of products and services with pricing options for your customers.
  • Multi-currency support: Support for multiple currencies allows you to sell products and services worldwide.
  • Search and filter: Make it easy for your customers to find the products and services they need with advanced search and filter options.
  • Analytics and reporting: Get insights into your product and service sales with analytics and reporting features.

Designed to help businesses manage their orders from start to finish

  • Customizable order forms: Create tailored order forms that match your business's unique requirements.
  • Automated workflows: Automate order fulfillment processes to streamline operations and reduce errors.
  • Shipping integration: Integrate with popular shipping carriers to calculate shipping costs and generate shipping labels.
  • Sales order tracking: Monitor the status of each order from creation to delivery.
  • Data analysis: Gain insights into sales trends and customer behavior with advanced reporting and analytics capabilities.

Power up your business with iCustomer!
