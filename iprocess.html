What is iProcess?

iProcess is a powerful yet easy-to-use tool that can help you automate a wide range of business processes. With iProcess, you can eliminate time-consuming manual tasks and streamline your workflows, allowing you to focus on what really matters – growing your business. Whether you need to automate simple tasks like data entry or more complex workflows that involve multiple departments and stakeholders, iProcess has you covered.

Rev Up Your Business Efficieny!

Securely integrate with external/3rd party systems

  • Template-based and Custome Connectors: Have seamless integration with external/3rd party systems.
  • Real-time Data Integration: Ensures that data is always up-to-date across all connected systems.
  • Easy Configuration: Configuring is straightforward and quick, saving your business valuable time and resources.
  • Data Security: Robust data security measures ensures that all data transfers are secure and compliant with relevant regulations.

Design, automate, and optimize your workflows to align with your business objectives

  • Drag-and-Drop Interface: Intuitive drag-and-drop designer interface.
  • Rule-Based Process Routing: Set up process routing rules, so that work is automatically routed to the right person or department.
  • Workflow Automation: Eliminate manual work and increase efficiency.
  • Real-Time Process Monitoring:> See the status of each process and quickly identify bottlenecks or issues.

Streamline Your Workflow with Custom Forms -  Without Writing A Single Line of code!

  • User-Friendly Interface: user-friendly allows you to create custom forms with ease and without any technical background or coding experience.
  • Custom Form Actions: Define actions to perform certain tasks based on the input provided by the user.
  • Workflow Integration: Easily integrate with your workflows to streamline your business processes.
  • Data Validation: Built-in data validation ensures that the data entered into the form is accurate and consistent.

Effortlessly store and reuse workflows with iProcess Process Repository

  • Save, store and reuse previously designed workflows
  • Share workflows with team members for collaboration
  • Easy access to workflow templates for faster process creation
  • Version control for easy tracking of changes and updates
