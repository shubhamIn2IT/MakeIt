What is Mapula?

Revolutionize Your Customer Experience with Mapula - The AI-Based Chatbot Framework!

Mapula is an Artificial Intelligence-based chatbot framework designed to enhance customer experience. It is a versatile solution that can respond to an array of questions across various industries. With its advanced features, Mapula can understand user sentiment, engage live assistants, and even recognize voice commands in the user's local language.

Here's what makes it unique:

  • Versatility: Mapula can respond to an array of questions across various industries, making it a versatile solution for businesses.
  • Advanced Features: With AI capabilities, Mapula understands user sentiment, engages live assistants, and even recognizes voice commands in the user's local language.
  • Fundamental Pillars: Mapula is built on three key pillars to ensure an exceptional customer experience:
    • Effectiveness: Delivering top-notch performance
    • Efficiency: Optimizing functionality while maintaining a human touch
    • Satisfaction: Prioritizing user affect, ethical behavior, and accessibility.

Maximize Your Chatbot's Performance with iDashboard

Monitor and optimize your chatbot's operations by tracking the key metrics that matter.

These metrics are divided into three main categories:

  • User Engagement Metrics: Understand how users are interacting with your chatbot, such as total number of sessions, session duration, and message response time.
