What is iSense?

iSense is an Event Aggregation and Automation platform that collects events from multiple sources, including APIs, SNMP, webhooks, and database connections.
It aggregates, normalizes, enriches, reduces noise, correlates events, and provides valuable insights to help you manage your IT operations.

Transform Your IT Operations with iSense Key Features and Simplify Your IT Management!

iSense offers a wide range of features to help you manage your IT operations more effectively.

Valuable insights through event collection and enrichment

  • Automated Event Collection: iSense automatically collects events from multiple sources, saving you time and effort.
  • Customizable Event Filtering: iSense allows you to customize filters to eliminate irrelevant alerts and focus on the events that matter.
  • Enriched Event Data: iSense enriches event data with context and insights, providing you with valuable information to make informed decisions.
  • Correlated Events: iSense correlates events across multiple systems and applications, identifying related issues and their impact on business services.

Advanced Root Cause Analysis with iSense Machine Learning

  • Deep-Dive Analysis: iSense provides deep-dive analysis to identify the root cause of complex issues, helping IT teams troubleshoot more effectively.
  • Real-time Visibility: iSense offers real-time visibility into the health of your IT environment, allowing you to quickly identify issues and their root causes.
  • Historical Trend Analysis: iSense analyzes historical trends to identify recurring issues and potential causes, helping you proactively prevent future incidents.
  • Collaborative Resolution: iSense facilitates collaborative resolution of issues by enabling teams to share data and collaborate on troubleshooting efforts.
  • Automated Remediation:> iSense offers automated remediation of common issues, enabling you to quickly resolve problems without manual intervention.

Proactive fault Management with Predictive Analytics

  • Proactive Maintenance: iSense helps you to identify potential issues before they cause any harm, allowing you to take corrective actions to prevent any downtime or outages.
  • Forecasting: iSense uses predictive analytics to forecast resource usage, providing you with insights into when you may need to scale up or down your resources.
  • Trend Analysis: iSense performs trend analysis on historical data to identify patterns and anomalies, allowing you to make data-driven decisions for improving your IT operations.
  • What-if Analysis: iSense allows you to simulate different scenarios and predict the impact of changes to your IT infrastructure, helping you make informed decisions and optimize resource allocation.

Automated Incident Creation with iSense

  • Configurable Rules: Create custom rules to automatically trigger incident creation based on event data.
  • Integration with ITSM Tools: iSense integrates with popular ITSM tools to automate the incident creation process.
  • Reduced Response Times: By automating incident creation, iSense helps reduce response times and resolve issues more quickly.
  • Simplified Event Management: iSense auto-incident creation feature simplifies event management by automating the incident creation process, freeing up IT teams to focus on more critical tasks.
